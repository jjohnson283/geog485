Milestone #13

James Johnson


With the recent devestating mudslide outside of Oso, Washington I thought I'd address the problem of landslides in the United States. Landslides occur all over the country yet there is not much done in the way of mitigatation. My map will show where the most risk of landslides are so people can see how widespread this natural and potentially devestating phenomenon is.

Included in this this map will be overlays of geologic phenomina that are related to landslides. Each overlay has a custom style except for the original WMS overlay.

Overlays that show geologic phenomena that cause landslide include:

  • Volcanoes-Shown by red circles
  • Earthquakes - Shown by blue squares

There are also overlays that show the incidence and susceptibility to landslides accross the U.S. as well as points where costly landslide events have taken place. The overlay labeled "Original WMS" is the one that corresponds to the legend below the map.

  • Costly Landslides - Shows the points of all landslides accross the country that were costly incidents - Represented by green triangles.
