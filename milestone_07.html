Given that McKinley County NM is contained within the following BBOX: -109.5, 34.5, -106.5, 36.5

What is the aspect ratio of this geographic region?

Aspect ratio=(maxx-minx)/(maxy-miny)

Aspect ratio=(-106.5-(-109.1))/(36.5-34.5)=2.6

Aspect ratio=2.6

height=width/aspect ratio

height=600/2.6

height=231 pixels (rounded up from 230.769)
